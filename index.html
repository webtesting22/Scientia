Regular Tests & Doubt Cleaance

Scientia offers 45 practice tests very similar to the actual SAT. We offer this series of SAT mock tests so that you can practice and score well. It is also important to review your performance in each practice test to identify your strenths/weaknesses and improve your score. Closer to each SAT exam, we offer proctored tests in the center in a test environment. The mock tests will give you a complete test experience and will prepare you for the actual SAT exam. The more you practice, the better your chances of getting a great score on your actual SAT.

SAT Prep

The Intensive Course is SCIENTIA's most popular preparatory program. The course preapares all students in grades 9-12 intending to take the SAT exam with a comprehensive blueprint to the Reading, Writing and Language, and Mathematics content on the exam, along with an in-depth and extensive guide to the new optional essay. SCIENTA's courses are designed to provide a balance of guided instruction, collaborative discussion and group work, and realistic practice opportunities. Students work both collaboratively and individually as they apply new strategies, with the instructor serving as an approachable mentor.
Each class includes:
• Engaging lecture, to introduce strategy and new content
• Collaborative problem-solving, to takcle difficult problems early in the course
• realistic mock test-taking, to ensure that students are well prepared for true test conditions
• Team-based competition, to make vocabulary building a lettle less tedious! Students receive the following evalutions and feedback
• Forty five 4-hour diagnostic tests: full-length tests taken under ralistic conditions
• Frequent vocavulary quizzing, in the form of a written quiz or team-based competition
• Daily homework review, to explain challenging questions and to reinforce new content
• Regular math, reading, and grammer review and practice
• Helpfull analysis and grading of minimum of 7 essays
• Individual final reports to help students plan their studying after the end of the course

AP tests allow you the chance to save money and get started on your degree before you've even reached college.
